# UI/UX Precision Blueprint (Apple Inspired)

**Source of Truth:** `multi-restaurant-design-ui.md`

## 1. Visual Rhythm & Atmosphere
- **Binary Section Rhythm:** Alternating between deep `Absolute Black (#000000)` chapters and `Pale Apple Gray (#f5f5f7)` merchandising fields.
- **The "Two Gears" System:**
    - **Showcase Mode:** Cinematic, large breathing room, foregrounded imagery.
    - **Transaction Mode:** High-density retail blocks, compressed spacing, actionable utility.

## 2. Granular Color Palette
### Primary & Surface
- `Absolute Black (#000000)`: Immersive canvases.
- `Pale Apple Gray (#f5f5f7)`: Main light surface.
- `Pure White Canvas (#ffffff)`: Transactional/Retail backgrounds.
- `Near-Black Ink (#1d1d1f)`: Primary text on light.

### Graphite Surfaces (Dark Mode / Overlays)
- `Graphite A (#272729)`: Dark card base.
- `Graphite B (#262629)`: Control groupings.
- `Graphite C (#28282b)`: Elevated dark surfaces.

### Interactive & Accents
- `Apple Action Blue (#0071e3)`: Primary buttons and focus.
- `Body Link Blue (#0066cc)`: Inline links.
- `High-Luminance Blue (#2997ff)`: Links on dark scenes.

## 4. Component Geometry & Depth
- **Radius Tiers:**
    - `5px`: Micro labels/tags.
    - `12px`: Standard buttons and input fields.
    - `18px`: Order/Product cards.
    - `56px+`: Signature "Capsule" CTA links.
- **Elevation Map:**
    - `Level 0`: Flat neutral surfaces (Tonal separation).
    - `Level 1`: `#d2d2d7` / `#86868b` border-led containment.
    - `Level 2`: `rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` soft shadow for active cards.
- **Touch Targets:** Minimum `44x44px` for all interactive elements.

## 5. Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile:** 375px (Single column, condensed selectors).
- **Tablet:** 641px (Mixed 1-2 column transitions).
- **Desktop:** 1024px (Full retail comparison grids).
- **Large Desktop:** 1441px+ (Maximum editorial breathing room).
