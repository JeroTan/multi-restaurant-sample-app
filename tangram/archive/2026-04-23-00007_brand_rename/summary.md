# Feature Summary: Brand Rename to Multi QR Ordering System

## Intent
To correct the branding across the application. The term "Tangram" is the name of our AI workflow/system, not the name of the app. The app should be rebranded to "Multi QR Ordering System" in all user-facing interfaces.

## Scope
- **Content Updates:** Scan and replace any hardcoded instances of the word "Tangram" with "Multi QR Ordering System" (or relevant generic phrasing) across all UI components and pages in the `src/` directory.

## Strategic Fit
This feature ensures the application's branding is accurate and professional for the end-user, correcting a misinterpretation of the internal AI workflow name.

## Final Execution Log
- **What was Built**: Rebranded the entire application by replacing all user-facing instances of "Tangram" with "Multi QR Ordering System" or generic terminology.
- **Challenges & Fixes**: No significant bugs. Verified the brand removal using recursive grep search.
- **Design Adherence**: Maintained the clean UI hierarchy and established architectural patterns.
