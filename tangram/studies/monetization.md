# Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Model
**SaaS Subscription (Tiered)**

## Implementation Logic
- **Basic Tier:** Standard ordering, single language, limited table count.
- **Pro Tier:** Advanced analytics, multi-language support, unlimited tables, and custom branding.
- **Sample App Implementation:** The app will include "Subscription Status" in the tenant model to demonstrate feature gating logic (e.g., restricted dashboard access if the subscription is "expired").

## Future Expansion
- **Transaction Fees:** Potential small commission on processed orders (post-MVP).
- **Premium Add-ons:** Loyalty programs, dedicated printer integrations, and marketing tools.
