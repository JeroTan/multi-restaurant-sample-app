# Project Goal

## Primary Objective
Demonstrate a functional end-to-end QR ordering flow in a multi-tenant environment to prove the technical viability of the SaaS model.

## Success Criteria
- **Functional Flow:** A customer can scan a QR code, browse a menu, and submit an order that appears instantly on a staff dashboard.
- **Multi-tenancy:** Data remains strictly isolated between different mock restaurants.
- **"Zero-Touch" Onboarding:** A restaurant admin can independently set up a menu and generate table-specific QR codes.
- **Edge-First Validation:** Successful deployment and operation on Cloudflare Workers/D1.
